\section{Statechart Diagrams}
\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.5\textwidth]{graphics/Statechart Diagram/ScureLogin.png}
    \caption{Statechart Diagram for Secure Login.}
\end{figure}
Lifecycle of a session: Unauthenticated → RedirectingToSSO → AwaitingCredentials → Authenticating. \\
From Authenticating: \\
\quad [SSO unreachable] → SSOError → End; \\
\quad [Invalid credentials] → AuthFail → AwaitingCredentials; \\
\quad [Valid] → ValidatingToken. \\
From ValidatingToken: \\
\quad [invalid] → Authenticating; \\
\quad [valid] → FetchingData. \\
From FetchingData: \\
\quad [multiple roles] → SelectingRole → ApplyingRBAC → Authenticated; \\
\quad [single role] → ApplyingRBAC → Authenticated.

\begin{figure}[H]

    \includegraphics[width=0.5\textwidth]{graphics/Statechart Diagram/UC003.png}
    \caption{Statechart Diagram for Check Schedule.}
\end{figure}
Lifecycle of a schedule: Draft → Submission → Validation. \\
At Validation: [no conflict] → AwaitingConfirmation → Confirmed → Saved → End; [conflict] → Blocked → End. \\
Exception branches: OutOfPolicy → Blocked; \\
SchedulerDelay → Pending → QueueResolved → Draft/Saved | Error → End; \\
TutorExit → Draft (unchanged).

Lifecycle of booking: Browsing → CheckingAvailability. \\
\quad [unavailable] → Browsing; \\
\quad [available] → AwaitConfirmation. \\
\quad [cancel] → Browsing; \\
\quad [confirm] → CreatingBookingRecord. \\
\quad [fail] → Browsing; \\
\quad [success] → NotifyingTutor. \\
\quad [Tutor accepts] → Confirmed → End; \\
\quad [Tutor rejects] → Rejected → End.

\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.5\textwidth]{graphics/Statechart Diagram/UC007_StateChartDiagram.png}
    \caption{Statechart Diagram for Modify Booking.}
\end{figure}
Lifecycle of a booking: Booked → ActionSelected (Reschedule | Cancel). 
Reschedule → CheckingAlternatives → [available] UpdatingSlots → Booked (new slot) | [none] Waitlist → End (notify-me). 
Cancel → Cancelled → SlotFreed. 
Exception branches: 
OutOfPolicy → Blocked → End; 
SchedulerDelay → Pending → QueueResolved → Booked/Cancelled | Error → End. 
StudentExit → Booked (unchanged).
\clearpage

    \caption{Statechart Diagram for At-Risk Student Detection.}
\end{figure}
Lifecycle of analysis session: Idle → FiltersConfigured → DataAggregation. \\
From DataAggregation: \\
\quad [stale] →RetrySync → DataAggregation; \\
\quad [fresh] → AnalysisRunning. \\
From AnalysisRunning: \\
\quad [success] → RiskListGenerated → [matches] StaffReview → ExportRequest → ExportComplete → Logged; \\
\quad [no matches] → EmptyList → StaffReview → ExportRequest → ExportComplete → Logged; \\
\quad [fail] → AnalysisError → Logged.
